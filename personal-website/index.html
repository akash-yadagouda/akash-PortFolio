About me

I'm a distributed systems engineer with a passion for building scalable, fault-tolerant systems that power modern applications. With expertise in Kafka, microservices, SQL databases, and Java, I focus on solving complex challenges in system design and data consistency.

Beyond engineering, I'm deeply invested in research and knowledge sharing. I regularly read foundational papers on distributed systems, study real-world implementations, and document my learnings through technical articles and guides. I believe in the power of sharing knowledge to elevate the entire engineering community.

My approach combines theoretical understanding with practical implementation, ensuring that every system I design is both elegant and production-ready.
